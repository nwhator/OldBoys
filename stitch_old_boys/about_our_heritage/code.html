The Old Boys' Chronicle

Founded on the principles of character and community, our institution began as a beacon of light in the heart of Lagos. For over a hundred years, the halls of our campus have echoed with the footsteps of Nigeria's brightest minds.

The "Old Boys" tradition is more than just a label; it is a lifelong commitment to the values of our heritage. From the early colonial architecture that still stands as a testament to our endurance, to the modern digital transformation of our classrooms, our history is a living document of progress.
